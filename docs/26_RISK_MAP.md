# 26 Risk Map / 风险地图

## 目标

Risk Map 将品牌、库存、供应商、合同、门店、现金流、人员和外部市场风险连接到知识图谱，帮助管理层看到风险关联。

## 页面与 API

- `/graph`
- `GET /api/graph/risk-map`
- `POST /api/graph/risk-map`
- `GET /api/graph/osprey-risk`

## Risk Types

- `price_risk`
- `inventory_risk`
- `supplier_risk`
- `contract_risk`
- `staff_risk`
- `store_risk`
- `brand_risk`
- `cashflow_risk`
- `external_market_risk`

## Osprey Risk Graph

Osprey risk graph is a structured placeholder until real linked data exists. It can connect brand, pricing risk, decision memory, market observation, inventory, supplier or agency, rebate uncertainty, tasks, documents and AI suggestions.

The system must not claim real financial numbers unless entered or synced from trusted data sources.
