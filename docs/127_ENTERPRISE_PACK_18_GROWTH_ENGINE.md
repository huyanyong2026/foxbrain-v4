# Enterprise Pack 18 - Growth Engine

## Goal

Pack 18 establishes the Enterprise Growth Engine as the unified growth analysis layer for
stores, brands, products and customers. Growth analysis uses the unified data model,
enterprise knowledge and Enterprise Decision Engine.

## Core Rule

All growth recommendations must be explainable and traceable to data sources.

## API Contracts

- `/api/growth-engine/framework`
- `/api/growth-engine/scorecard`
- `/api/growth-engine/store-growth`
- `/api/growth-engine/brand-product`
- `/api/growth-engine/customer-growth`
- `/api/growth-engine/executive-scorecard`

## Growth Scorecard

Score dimensions:

- Revenue
- Margin
- Customer
- Operations
- Learning
- Innovation

Each score includes supporting basis from metrics, KPI catalog, decision engine,
university, strategy center or digital twin.

## Store Growth

Store growth evaluates:

- Sales trends
- Store maturity
- Expansion potential
- Improvement opportunities

## Brand and Product Growth

Brand and product growth tracks:

- Category growth
- Brand contribution
- Product lifecycle
- Promotion effectiveness

## Customer Growth

Customer growth supports:

- Member segmentation
- Loyalty analysis
- Churn prediction
- Campaign recommendations
