# 44 Customer Growth Engine

## Goal

Customer Growth Engine helps manage members, customer segments, tags, follow-ups, events and repeat purchase opportunities.

## Page

- `/customer-growth`

## Models

- `customer_segments`
- `customer_tags`
- `customer_followups`
- `customer_events`

## Segments

Default strategy templates include new customers, old customers, VIP customers, high value customers, dormant customers, hiking, camping, running, price sensitive and high-end equipment customers.

## Safety

No customer profile, purchase history or value conclusion should be invented. Customer privacy has priority.
